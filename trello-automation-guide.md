# How to Automate Card Movement in Trello
*Save time by automatically moving cards to "Done" when all checklist items are completed.*

## Prerequisites
* A **Trello** account.
* A board with at least two lists: **In Progress** and **Done**.

## 1. Open the Butler Automation Tool
1. Open your Trello board.
2. Click **Automation** in the top header.
3. Select **Rules** from the dropdown menu.
4. Click **Create Rule** in the top-right corner.

## 2. Set the "Checklist Complete" Trigger
To tell Trello when to act:
1. Click **+ Add Trigger**.
2. Select the **Checklists** tab.
3. Choose the option: "When a checklist is **completed** in a card."
4. Click the green **+** icon to save the trigger.

> **Tip:** You can specify a specific checklist name (like "Quality Check") to make the rule even more precise.

## 3. Add the "Move to Done" Action
1. Select the **Move** tab in the actions menu.
2. Choose **move the card to the top of list "Done"**.
3. Click the green **+** icon to add the action.
4. Click **Save** at the top of the page.
