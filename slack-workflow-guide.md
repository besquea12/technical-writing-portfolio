markdown \# How to Automate a "Welcome" Message in Slack *Improve team
onboarding by automatically greeting new members in specific channels.*

## Prerequisites

- A **Slack** workspace with a Pro, Business+, or Enterprise Grid plan.
- **Builder** permissions (usually enabled by default for members).

## 1. Open the Workflow Builder

1.  Open Slack on your desktop.
2.  Click your workspace name in the top left.
3.  Select **Tools** \> **Workflow Builder**.
4.  Click the **Create** button in the top right corner.

## 2. Set the "New Member" Trigger

To start the automation when someone joins: 1. Select **Standard** as
your workflow type. 2. Choose **New member joins a channel** from the
trigger list. 3. Select the specific channel (e.g., `#general` or
`#onboarding`).

> **Note:** You can only set triggers for public channels unless you are
> already a member of the private channel.

## 3. Add the Automated Message

1.  Click **Add Step**.
2.  Select **Send a message**.
3.  In the "Send this message to" field, select **The person who joined
    the channel**.
4.  Type your welcome text (e.g., "Welcome to the team! Check the pinned
    posts for our handbook.")
5.  Click **Save** and then **Publish**.
