# How to Create a Shared Team Wiki in Notion
*Consolidate your team's knowledge into a single, searchable database.*

## Prerequisites
* An active **Notion** account.
* Workspace **Admin** or **Member** permissions.

## 1. Create the Wiki Database
1. In the left sidebar, click **+ Add a page**. ![Add a page] (https://photos.app.goo.gl/bm4r3TckorkteV6K7)
2. Click **+ Empty Database** in the setup menu. !["empty database"] (https://photos.app.goo.gl/zijzf5cqWfhaGG1x6)
3. Select **Table** from the list of view options. !["Table"] (https://photos.app.goo.gl/yLgmSyFZdgHVtWPv7)
4. Title the page "Team Wiki." !["Team Wiki"] https://photos.app.goo.gl/sDRKB4K8Xr55ovJL6

## 2. Customize Your Categories
To keep the wiki organized, add tags to your entries:
1. Select **Add Page** on th left to create a list. !["Add page on the bottom left"] (https://photos.app.goo.gl/sDRKB4K8Xr55ovJL6)
2. Add options like `HR`, `Engineering`, `Marketing`, and `Onboarding`.
3. To add texts, URL, links, click on **Add property** on the left side. 
4. Click on the **Settings slider** next to the **New template option** at the top right. 
5. Select **Edit Property** to edit a specific property. ![ "Edit property"] https://photos.app.goo.gl/fgq1ZJDsACenbP7cA

> **Tip:** Use high-contrast colors for tags to make them easier to scan quickly.

## 3. Share with Your Team
1. Click the **Share** button in the top-right corner. https://photos.app.goo.gl/JbvcLEm9z2XuFfnU7
3. Type the email addresses of your team members.
4. Set permissions to **Can view** or **Can edit**.
5. Click **Invite**.
